Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15.11.2024

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data or require any form of user authentication to use the App. However, we use third-party services that may collect data to provide insights and improve the App's functionality.

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. This service may collect anonymized usage data such as:

  • Session duration
  • Pages/screens viewed
  • App interactions

This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

Firebase Crashlytics

Firebase Crashlytics helps us identify and fix issues by collecting crash logs. This information includes:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and states leading to a crash

No personally identifiable information is collected.

How We Use the Information

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Improving the App's user experience and functionality
  • Debugging and resolving technical issues

Sharing of Information

We do not share or sell your information to third parties. However, Firebase services may process and store the data they collect in accordance with their privacy policy.

Data Security

We prioritize protecting your data. While we use trusted third-party services to safeguard information, we cannot guarantee absolute security due to the nature of online data transmission.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
